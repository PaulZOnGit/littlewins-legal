# Privacy Policy for LittleWins
Last updated: 18 May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the LittleWins mobile application ("the App") provided by Paul Züge ("I", "me") as a free service.

## Summary
LittleWins runs entirely on your device. I do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, usage data, or analytics. The App does not contain advertising, tracking SDKs (Software Development Kits), or accounts.

## Data the App collects
None. All data you create in the App stays on your device.

## Data the App shares
None.

## Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:
- Notifications — to deliver scheduled reminders you set for your habits. The app reschedules local notifications on device restart. You can decline this at any time in iOS Settings → Notifications → LittleWins, or revoke it later under Settings → LittleWins.

## Third-party services
The App contains a single link to a Google Forms page used for feedback. If you choose to follow that link and submit feedback, you leave the App and Google's privacy policy applies to that form: https://policies.google.com/privacy. Submitting feedback is entirely optional.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and TestFlight, and runs on top of Apple's operating system and services. Apple may independently collect data (such as crash reports, performance metrics, and installation data) under its own privacy policy, outside of my control. See https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

## Tracking
The App does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, and does not use the App Tracking Transparency framework because it performs no tracking as defined by Apple.

## Children's privacy
The App is not directed at children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Your rights under GDPR
Because no personal data is collected, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you have any questions, you may contact me at littlewinsapp@gmail.com.

## Changes to this policy
If I change how the App handles data in the future (for example, by adding anonymous usage analytics), I will update this policy and the privacy declarations in App Store Connect before the change takes effect.
